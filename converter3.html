Tips
  • Make sure to purchase the type of mulch that meets your needs. For example, purchase a less coarse mulch for playground areas.

  • Make sure to apply the proper amount of mulch suitable for your project. For example, to prevent weed growth in garden or raised bed areas you should apply enough so the depth of the mulch is two to four inches.

  • Apply a new layer of organic mulch every few years to make up for break down and dispersal.

  • Use proper equipment for transporting and applying mulch.
