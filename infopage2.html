Tips
  • Make sure your material is properly grounded or else it will prevent successful welds.

  • Make sure that the wire at the tip of the welding gun is at or a little less than a quarter of an inch in length to prevent incorrect welds.

  • Make sure to keep the tip of the welding gun clean and free from dirt/debris.

  • Make sure to wear proper eye protection, jacket, gloves, pants, etc. Also, make sure that the area you are working in is well ventilated.
